bool LootLocker.LootLockerPresenceManager.AutoDisconnectOnFocusChange
staticgetset

Whether presence should automatically disconnect when the application loses focus or is paused.

When enabled, presence will disconnect when the app goes to background and reconnect when it returns to foreground. Useful for saving battery on mobile or managing resources.
