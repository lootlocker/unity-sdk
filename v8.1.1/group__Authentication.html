Authentication

Detailed Description

Start and end player sessions via Guest, Platform, or White Label login.

Three login families are supported:

  • Guest Login — assigns each player a unique device-local ID; no account required. Ideal for fast entry on mobile or web.
  • Platform Login — delegates to a first-party platform (Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, Epic Games, Google Play, Apple Game Center, Meta/Oculus, Amazon Luna, Discord).
  • White Label Login — custom email/password accounts for your game.

See also White Label Login and Connected Accounts.

See the LootLocker documentation.
